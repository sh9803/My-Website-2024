ALL ABOUT DOGS!

5 fun facts about dogs you don't already know are:

  • Dogs can smell fear! Dogs have olfactory superpowers that can detect a human's emotional state by the scent that a human emits.

  • A dog's nose is it's fingerprint! No two dogs have the same nose. Each has a unique pattern to the individual.

  • All puppies are born deaf. However, they can hear four times better as they age than humans.

  • Dogs have dreams too. Scientific evidence, based on brain wave patterns, suggests that dogs dream just like us.

  • Dogs have three eyelids. Many owners haven't heard of this interesting dog fact, but did you know that your four-legged friend has three eyelids?

    • 3 interesting dog breeds are:

    MALTIPOO:

    A Maltipoo is a cute little dog that's a cross of a Maltese and a Toy or Miniature Poodle. Clever, playful, and affectionate, Maltipoos retain their puppy-like looks and behavior well into their teen years. Their coat can be scruffy or curly and comes in a variety of colors, although it is most often white or cream.

    PUG:

    Pugs have been around since at least 400 B.C. The jolly little dogs were always bred as companions, so they make ideal pets for everyone, from solo apartment dwellers in cities to families in the suburbs. Pugs are especially known for their cheerful dispositions and friendly nature.

    SUSSEX SPANIEL:

    Sussex Spaniels form strong bonds with their humans, they don’t fare so well when left alone for long periods of time. As a sensitive breed, they crave affection and attention. They require firm but gentle leadership during training.
